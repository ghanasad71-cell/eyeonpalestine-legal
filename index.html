Telegram → TikTok Automation

Automate posting from Telegram to TikTok effortlessly

About the App

This app connects your Telegram channels or groups to TikTok, allowing you to automatically post videos, images, and captions from Telegram to your TikTok account.

How It Works

  • Connect your Telegram account
  • Choose channels or groups to pull content from
  • Filter, edit, and prepare posts automatically
  • Upload directly to TikTok using the TikTok API

Privacy Policy

We do not collect personal information beyond what is required to connect your accounts and post content. All data remains private and secure.

Terms of Service

By using this app, you agree to only post content that complies with Telegram and TikTok’s community guidelines.
